About iamNu

iamNU is a project that started about three and a half years ago during the peak of Dance Music. I remember the first event I ever attended - a Swedish House Mafia show. The production was huge and I fell in love with it and the culture instantly. I was exposed to dance music early on, somewhere in the early 2000's where I'd pick up CD's of what was then-called Techno. Featuring artists like, Cascada, Darude, and Modjo.

I knew from that day I wanted to see what it was like to give people who love dance music the feeling that the DJs were giving me during that show and so through trial and error I self-taught myself how to DJ in my bedroom. Finding ways to standout from others, I tried digging deeper into the internet to find tracks that were more deep house, garage house, bass house, and breaks. I wanted to stray away from playing the "biggest EDM tracks" of the years. And with that, came iamNu. Over the course of these three years, I've played many shows in the South Bay and I'm hoping to branch out into other parts of the Bay Area. Let's see where this journey will take me!
